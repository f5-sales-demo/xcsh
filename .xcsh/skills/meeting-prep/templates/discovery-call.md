## Agenda

### 1. Introductions and Objectives (5 min)
- Introduce team
- State meeting purpose: understand {{customer}}'s environment and challenges
- Confirm agenda and time

### 2. Customer Environment Overview (10 min)
- Current infrastructure landscape
- Application architecture (cloud, on-prem, hybrid)
- Existing security and networking stack
- Team structure and operational model

### 3. Pain Point Deep-Dive (15 min)
- What challenges prompted this conversation?
- Impact on the business (quantified if possible)
- What has been tried before?
- Timeline and urgency drivers

### 4. F5 XC Capability Alignment (10 min)
- Map customer pain to F5 XC capabilities
- Architecture-level fit (not feature list)
- Reference similar deployments

### 5. Next Steps (5 min)
- Technical deep-dive scheduling
- Access and environment requirements
- Action items with owners and dates
